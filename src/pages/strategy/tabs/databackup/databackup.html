Why my backup/import always freezes forever, or finnaly gives me an 'Out of Memory' error?
Unfortunately, quantities of your history records have been increased too large to be handled by browser within limited memory space.
Try backup v2 method instead, if possible, when you get such issue on backup v1 method.
Alternatively, there is another method to backup your data by manually copying browser files from profile folders to your own folders, could be tried.

    Backup v1

  • Export data to a file(.kc3data/.zip)
  • Import data from a file(.kc3data/.zip)
    • Read the WARNINGS first!
      • DO NOT go to other tabs while importing your data.
      • DO NOT close the window while importing your data.
      • DO NOT input other files.
      • Especially large files may take over an hour to finish. BE PATIENT.
    • Select file:
    • Overwrite data

    Backup v2

    On starting the v2 backup process, you will be asked to select the backup folder. Then, you will be asked for permission (twice) to view and write the folder.

  • Create new backup
  • Update existed backup
  • Restore data from backup

    • Read following notes and the WARNINGS first!

    • DO NOT go to other tabs while importing your data.
    • DO NOT close the window while importing your data.
    • DO NOT input other files.
    • Especially large files may take over an hour to finish. BE PATIENT.

    This is an experimental backup feature that provides more stability at the cost of speed.
    It also allows for updating the existed backup files without having to perform a full backup.

    Please note that it is only supported by Chrome 86 and later.
    The v2 backup files are NOT compatible with v1 backup, and vice versa. Don't mix them up.

Please be patient.
This process may take a long time.
DO NOT TOUCH ANYTHING
BEFORE THE "Finished!" MESSAGE!!!
